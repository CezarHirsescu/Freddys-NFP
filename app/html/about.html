About Our Organization

Climate and Real Environment (C.A.R.E) is a non-profit organization that was founded mid-August of 2021 to help spread and raise awareness on the rising sea levels on the coasts of the pacific islands. Our final goal being to raise enough money to support environmental teams to reduce gas emissions and make the pacific area a greener and cleaner resort. C.A.R.E is accepting donations from anyone willing to help this environmental mission. As a team, we stand by making the pacific a more eco-friendly and more climate controlled area. to ensure that the aquatic life and those living near the body of water can live without any disturbances.
